Pre-departure and Post-arrival Services

Savina makes sure that you do not have a tough time trying to adapt to changes during, before, and after travel by offering the following services to our clients:

Pre-Departure Services:

  1. Visa Assistance: Our experienced consultants guide you through the visa application process, providing comprehensive support from documentation to embassy appointments.
  2. Travel Insurance: Protect your journey with our tailored travel insurance packages covering medical emergencies, trip cancellations, and unforeseen events.
  3. Accommodation Arrangements: Find safe and comfortable accommodation options near your educational institution with our assistance, whether it's homestays, dormitories, or rental apartments.
  4. Orientation Sessions: Prepare for your study abroad experience with informative sessions covering the destination country's culture, customs, and academic environment.
  5. Health and Safety Guidance: Stay healthy and safe abroad with our guidance on health precautions, vaccinations, and accessing medical services.

Post-Arrival Services:

  1. Airport Pickup: Upon arrival, enjoy seamless transportation from the airport to your accommodation with our welcoming representatives.
  2. Settling-in Assistance: Ease into your new environment with support in setting up bank accounts, navigating local transportation, and more.
  3. Local Support Services: Access ongoing support throughout your stay from our local representatives, assisting with academic, cultural, and logistical matters.
  4. Cultural Integration Activities: Immerse yourself in the host country's culture through language exchange programs, guided excursions, and other enriching activities.
  5. Emergency Assistance: Rest assured with 24/7 emergency assistance available for medical emergencies, logistical challenges, or any unforeseen situations.
